About

I have been teaching in Fort Worth, TX for the last several years, and have always wanted a simpler way of putting my lessons online. I wanted a website that didn't have a million features, something that was just easy to use. I also didn't want to spend time making the website look good - it should already be great looking, without any effort on my part

In the spring of 2013, I decided to create my own platform. That project has grown to what you see here today, on lessonwell.com. Lessonwell is my effort to provide fellow teachers with a dead-simple platform for hosting their lessons

I hope lessonwell saves you time and lets you focus on what we all appreciate you for: teaching our students. Thank you for all that you do, and thank you for your support of this little project of mine

Quentin Donnellan
Founder, lessonwell
