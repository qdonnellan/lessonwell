Lessonwell & Google

We use Google authentication for sign-in/sign-out in an effort to make your password management easier and more secure. Why does this matter to you?

  1. You don't have to remember a new username or password
  2. You don't have to worry about lessonwell losing your password (we never know what it is)
  3. Your account is secured by Google. This matters because we are a tiny company with no cryptography experts on board!

Any of the following Google Apps products will work properly with our system.

  1. Google Apps for Education
  2. Google Apps for Business
  3. Traditional Google Account (Gmail, Drive, etc.)

Essentially, if you have access to Gmail, Drive, or any other google service that you log in to access, you may use that account to authenticate to lessonwell.
